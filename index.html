The Startup Imperative: Why Cloud Stacking is Essential

A startup cannot afford the upfront capital expenditure of traditional data centers. Cloud Stacking allows a startup to build, deploy, and scale its product using flexible Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS).

Building a Lean Tech Stack with CloudStack

For startups prioritizing control, customization, and cost-efficiency over vendor lock-in, building a private or hybrid cloud with Apache CloudStack offers significant advantages:

1. Zero-Cost Infrastructure Orchestration

CloudStack is open-source. A startup can manage its entire IaaS layer—from compute (VMs) and networking to storage—without paying licensing fees, ensuring a true lean startup approach.

2. Multi-Hypervisor Flexibility

CloudStack supports various hypervisors (KVM, VMware, etc.), allowing startups to choose the best technology for different workloads without redesigning the entire management plane.
